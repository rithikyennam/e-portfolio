DRAG AND DROP QUIZ

I made changes to the given drag-and-drop quiz to make it look and feel better. To start off, I updated the the page title and contents. On the CSS, I enhanced the overall appearance by changing the font, background color, and styling of various elements. I also made the footer more visible, adjusted the quiz container's width, and centered it on the page. Furthermore, I improved the appearance of draggable items by changing their style, border color, padding, and margins. I also made small boxes in the right column more visually appealing and aligned them properly. Lastly, I modified the drag container and drag drop indicator for a more modern appearance, changing their background color and border style. To host it, I chose Github. I uploaded the files to github and then I enabled static hosting on it, which lead to a seamless deployment.
