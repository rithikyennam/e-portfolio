Educational Video

The creative journey was done using iMovie Maker application on my Mac, which facilitated a seamless and engaging storytelling process. To elevate viewer experience, I incorporated background music. For the voice, I leveraged the Mac Text-to-Voice feature which I seamlessly integrated it into the iMovie maker. I used smooth transitions to connect images, for a consistent visual story. Making the video was more enjoyable than I expected. When it all came together at the end, I felt really satisfied with it.
