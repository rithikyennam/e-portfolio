Screencast Tutorial

Checkout out the Screencast Tutorial on "How to set up automatic replies". I created this using the trial version of Camtasia 2023. In the video, I demonstrated how to set up automatic replies in Gmail. The process began with downloading the Camtasia application and initiating the screen recording feature to capture the entire tutorial. Once the recording phase was complete, I used built-in editing features of the Camtasia app. I incorporated various effects, such as blur to highlight specific areas, cursor magnification to draw attention to important clicks, and a selection of smooth transitions to enhance the overall viewing experience. After completing the editing process, I was able to export the video directly from the app to YouTube.
